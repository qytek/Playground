# Level 4: "The Abandoned Office"

**Survival Difficulty: Class 1**
* Safe
* Secure
* Minimal Entity Count

---

## 🏢 Environment
Level 4 resembles an infinite, empty modern office building. It is widely considered one of the best places in the Backrooms to rest, recuperate, and gather supplies.

* **Appearance:** Features barren rooms, cubicles, gray carpeting, and white walls.
* **Atmosphere:** Distant, low hum of fluorescent lights; mostly quiet and calm.
* **Geometry:** Non-linear layout. Walking backward rarely brings you to the same room.

## 💧 Resources
This level is highly valued by wanderers due to its abundance of life-saving supplies.

* **Almond Water:** Found in water coolers, drinking fountains, and vending machines.
* **Food:** Vending machines occasionally dispense snacks and energy bars.
* **Tools:** Office supplies, chairs, and occasional leftover electronics can be found.

## 🚫 Hazards & Entities
While Level 4 is exceptionally safe, it is not completely free of danger.

* **Entities:** Hostile creatures are incredibly rare. You may occasionally encounter a Hound or a Dullers, but they are easily avoided.
* **The Windows:** **Do not look out or approach windows.** Any window showing a landscape, darkness, or a figure is a trap designed to pull you through.

## 🤝 Communities & Outposts
Due to the safety and resource density, major factions maintain a permanent presence here.

### M.E.G. (Major Explorer Group) - Base Omega
* **Population:** Around 200 permanent residents.
* **Purpose:** The primary storage facility and medical outpost for the M.E.G.
* **Policy:** Open to traders and peaceful wanderers looking for medical aid.

### Amorphous Alchemy
* **Population:** Around 30 members.
* **Purpose:** A group of researchers dedicated to studying the chemical properties of items found in the Backrooms.
* **Policy:** Generally friendly, but highly secretive about their experiments.

## 🚪 Entrances & Exits

### How to Enter:
* **From Level 3:** Follow the signs pointing toward the "Office Sector" or climb up specific maintenance stairs.
* **From Level 2:** Enter through unlocked office doors found in the maintenance tunnels.

### How to Exit:
* **To Level 5:** Find an elevator shaft or follow a grand staircase downward.
* **To Level 6:** Wander into the rare, completely unlit sectors of the office.
* **To Level 37 (The Hub):** Certain doors marked with exit signs will lead directly into the Hub network.
